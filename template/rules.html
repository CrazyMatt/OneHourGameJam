Rules

One hour game jam takes place every Saturday at 20:00 UTC. At the start of the hour, a theme is announced in the IRC channel. You then have about an hour (we give you some flexibility) to submit a link to your game here on this website. You are free to use premade assets but it is recommended that you do make at least one game from scratch.

Then at about 21:30 UTC the bitslap brothers will stream themselves playing each of the games on twitch.tv/bitslap. The stream usually lasts about 2 hours.
